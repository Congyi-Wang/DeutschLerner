# German Language Curriculum Research

## CEFR Levels Overview

| Level | Words | Hours | Description |
|-------|-------|-------|-------------|
| **A1** | 500-800 | 60-80 | Survival: greetings, numbers, basic daily needs |
| **A2** | 1,000-1,500 | 150-200 | Everyday: shopping, travel, work, opinions |
| **B1** | 2,000-2,500 | 350-400 | Functional: travel situations, familiar topics |
| **B2** | 4,000-5,000 | 500-600 | Professional: complex texts, abstract topics |
| **C1** | 8,000-10,000 | 700-800 | Academic: nuanced, idiomatic expression |
| **C2** | 16,000+ | 1,000+ | Near-native: literary, technical, rare words |

## A1 Level — Detailed Breakdown

### Module Progression (based on Menschen, Schritte, Netzwerk textbooks)

**Module 1: Introductions & Personal Info (Week 1-2)**
- Greetings (Hallo, Guten Tag/Morgen/Abend, Tschüss, Auf Wiedersehen)
- Name, age, nationality, origin (Ich heiße..., Ich komme aus...)
- Alphabet & pronunciation rules
- Numbers 0-20
- Grammar: verb "sein", personal pronouns, W-questions (Wer? Was? Wo? Woher?)

**Module 2: Numbers, Dates & Daily Routines (Week 3-4)**
- Numbers to 100+
- Days, months, telling time
- Daily schedule verbs (aufstehen, frühstücken, arbeiten, schlafen)
- Grammar: der/die/das, ein/eine, present tense regular verbs, negation (nicht/kein)

**Module 3: Family, Hobbies & Free Time (Week 5-6)**
- Family members (Mutter, Vater, Bruder, Schwester...)
- Hobbies (spielen, lesen, schwimmen, kochen...)
- Likes/dislikes
- Grammar: possessive pronouns (mein/dein), separable verbs, modal verbs (können/mögen), accusative intro

**Module 4: Shopping, Food & Directions (Week 7-8)**
- Food & drink vocabulary
- Ordering at restaurants, grocery shopping
- Prices, currencies
- Asking/giving directions
- Grammar: accusative case with articles, place prepositions (in, auf, neben)

**Module 5: Housing, Travel & Transport (Week 9-10)**
- Rooms, furniture
- Transportation types
- Buying tickets, travel plans
- Grammar: movement prepositions (nach, zu, in), conjunctions (und, aber, oder), dative intro

**Module 6: Health, Weather & Everyday Life (Week 11-12)**
- Body parts, health phrases
- Weather expressions
- Clothing
- Grammar: imperatives, modal verbs (dürfen/müssen), adjectives, Perfekt introduction

### A1 Grammar Progression (Teaching Order)

1. Personal pronouns (ich, du, er, sie, es, wir, ihr, sie, Sie)
2. Verb "sein" (to be) conjugation
3. Verb "haben" (to have) conjugation
4. Regular verb conjugation — present tense
5. W-questions & yes/no questions
6. Noun genders (der/die/das)
7. Definite & indefinite articles
8. Negation (nicht / kein)
9. Plural forms
10. Numbers (cardinal & ordinal)
11. Possessive articles (mein, dein, sein, ihr...)
12. Nominative case
13. Accusative case (der→den, ein→einen)
14. Prepositions with accusative (für, ohne, durch, gegen, um)
15. Modal verbs (können, müssen, wollen, mögen, dürfen, sollen)
16. Separable verbs (aufstehen, einkaufen, fernsehen)
17. Word order (SVO, verb-second, questions)
18. Imperative mood
19. Perfekt introduction (Ich habe gemacht)
20. Basic prepositions of location/time

### A1 Vocabulary Categories (Teaching Order)

**Phase 1 — Absolute Basics (Week 1-2, ~80 words)**
- Greetings & farewells
- Personal information (name, age, address, phone)
- Countries & nationalities
- Numbers 0-100
- Alphabet & spelling

**Phase 2 — People & Relationships (Week 3-4, ~60 words)**
- Family members
- Professions
- Basic appearance/personality (groß, klein, jung, alt, nett)

**Phase 3 — Time & Routine (Week 5-6, ~70 words)**
- Days, months, seasons
- Time expressions (Uhr, Stunde, morgens, abends, heute, morgen)
- Daily routine verbs

**Phase 4 — Living & Environment (Week 7-8, ~60 words)**
- Housing (Wohnung, Zimmer, Küche, Bad...)
- Furniture & objects
- Colors

**Phase 5 — Food & Shopping (Week 9-10, ~80 words)**
- Food items
- Drinks
- Shopping vocabulary (kaufen, kosten, teuer, billig)
- Restaurant vocabulary (bestellen, Rechnung, Speisekarte)

**Phase 6 — Getting Around (Week 11-12, ~70 words)**
- Transport (Bus, Bahn, U-Bahn, Fahrrad, Auto)
- City & directions (links, rechts, geradeaus)
- Travel vocabulary

**Phase 7 — Body & Health (Week 13-14, ~60 words)**
- Body parts
- Health & doctor visits
- Weather expressions

**Phase 8 — Supplementary (~70 words)**
- Hobbies & leisure
- Clothing
- Common adjectives
- Question words

**Total A1 target: ~550-650 words**

## A2 Grammar Topics (for future reference)

1. Perfekt tense (haben + sein auxiliaries, regular/irregular participles)
2. Präteritum (simple past for sein/haben/modals)
3. Dative case fully developed
4. Two-way prepositions (Wechselpräpositionen)
5. Reflexive verbs
6. Comparatives & superlatives
7. Adjective declension
8. Subordinate clauses (weil, dass, wenn, obwohl)
9. Indirect questions
10. Konjunktiv II basics (würde + infinitive)
11. Verbs with prepositions
12. Relative clauses intro
13. Passive voice intro

## Best Practices for Language Learning Apps

1. **Spaced Repetition (SRS)** — most evidence-backed technique for vocab retention
2. **Context over isolation** — words in sentences > word-translation pairs
3. **Always show articles** — der/die/das with every noun, always
4. **Short daily sessions** — 10-20 min daily beats 1 hour weekly
5. **70/30 rule** — 70% review, 30% new material
6. **Active recall** — production (typing/speaking) > passive recognition
7. **Grammar in context** — teach grammar when needed for communication, not as abstract rules
8. **Progressive difficulty** — strict A1→A2→B1 level gating
9. **Communicative goals** — each lesson tied to a real-life scenario ("order food", "describe family")
10. **Cultural notes** — du vs. Sie, greeting customs, regional differences

## Reference Textbook Structures

### Menschen (Hueber) — A1
24 lessons in 8 modules of 3:
L1 Hallo! Ich bin Nicole → L6 Ich brauche kein Büro → L12 Was ist denn hier passiert? → L18 Geben Sie ihm diesen Tee → L24 Ich würde am liebsten feiern

### Schritte Plus Neu (Hueber) — A1
14 lessons: Greetings → Family → Shopping → My Apartment → My Day → Leisure → School → Work → Food → Health → Transport → Travel...

### Netzwerk Neu (Klett) — A1
12 chapters: Guten Tag! → Friends/Colleagues → In the City → Food → Daily Life → Time with Friends → Housing → Clothing → Travel → Vacation

**Common pattern:** All start with greetings/introductions, progress through daily life, end with travel/health.
